About CorroTech

CorroTech is an international company specializing in the supply of hard-to-source piping components for oil and gas, petrochemical, chemical, pharmaceutical, food and marine enterprises worldwide in America, Canada, Europe and the EAEU countries.

CorroTech: Your operational bridge to Chinese manufacturing.

1. End-to-end project involvement — from design to delivery.
We engage at the engineering stage: assist with material selection (titanium, stainless steel, duplex), adapt designs to meet ASME, EJMA requirements, coordinate drawings with factories, and provide seamless technical oversight — from your first inquiry to final handover at your warehouse.

2. Competitive pricing — thanks to long-term contracts with leading specialized Chinese manufacturers of pipe fittings, flanges, expansion joints, pipes, heat exchangers, glass-lined reactors, and custom industrial solutions.

Even for small orders, our high-volume production commitments grant us top-tier pricing.
This means:
→ You receive market or below-market pricing — even for trial or single-batch orders.
→ Price is fixed at quotation stage, with no hidden markups during shipping or customs clearance.

3. Speed — not as a promise, but as a production reality.
When deadlines are tight:
→ Thanks to our continuous production runs, we can integrate your order into an existing manufacturing cycle.
→ This is not “rush production at double cost,” but efficient scheduling — often without price increase and full compliance with API, ISO standards.
→ Lead times from confirmation are realistic and measurable.

4. Quality control. Our representatives are on-site at production facilities.
→ Every batch undergoes visual, dimensional, and documentation inspection before packaging.
→ Upon request: photo reports, video inspections, factory test protocols.
→ We do not ship until we verify quality ourselves.

5. Logistics to your door.
→ We manage the entire chain: from factory loading to delivery at your Russian facility.
→ We work with reliable freight forwarders, so we include backup time in the schedule in advance.
→ Full set of supporting documentation included.
